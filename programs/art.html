Description

The Meditative Art and Music program helps children develop self-awareness and improve their ability to express themselves. The art guides the discovery of the hidden beauty in the depths of children’s’ hearts and souls, while music merges in to unravel this rich world within each child.

The Meditative Art is the process of exploring personal creative potential. It is a way out of "I cannot" towards trusting yourself. Visual arts, in harmony with classical music, helps children express their feelings and experiences in the language of colours and images. The techniques of Meditative Art are especially valuable for autistic kids, because these kinds much better orient themselves in the language of shapes, images and colours, than in the language of words. The Meditative Art program hones children’s ability of self-expression using creative visual forms.

The Meditative Music and Art classes changed my boys (3 and 5 year old) significantly. It was a great pleasure to witness their transformation – they have blossomed like flowers that received much needed water. On a personal level, the tremendous experience of observing my children’s excitement about their own creations, taught me a lot about my children and the depth of their being. The mutual respect and freedom was the biggest gift I received from my sons. I am very thankful to the teachers of the Meditative Music and Art classes for supporting this oasis of creativity and independence for my children.
