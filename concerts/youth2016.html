Is it Shakespeare?
Is it a play?
No, it’s a classical music concert to gladden your heart!
Young performers will delight all audiences with the fruits of their musical talents. Solos, ensembles, duos, trios and much more. "All the world's a stage" as we align in spirit and experience the power of music in unison.

Our Annual Youth-for-Youth Classical Music Event will feature:

  • Classical music performed by youth from selected works by Mozart, Chopin, Schubert, Brahms, Renie, Gardiner, Lavignac, Strauss, Rossini, Copland and Bach-Gounod
  • Arts & Crafts Bazaar for Charity (interested participants please call Tania 647-825-2323)
  • Baking Contest for Youth (interested participants please call Victoria 416-998-7983)

The harmony of life will festively resonate within as we embark on the journey of music together!

The "Youth-for-Youth" concert series has started in 2015 with our first "Youth-for-Kids" concert, and will continue as a regular part of our annual event calendar. So "awake, dear heart, awake!" and experience the art of real life in music and harmony!
