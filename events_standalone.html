Mindfulness Program: Art of Living Without Stress The Art of Life Community Health Centre

Call for into regarding the next FREE Introductory class.

This 8-week course guided by an experienced Mindfulness Instructor Sabina Pillai, MA will help you to:

  • reduce your reaction to stress
  • get rid of anxiety and depression
  • enhance your memory & concentration skills, patience & personal performance
  • cultivate the skills of empathy and impulse control
  • sharpen your awareness, gradually improving every aspect of your life

Program designed at UMASS Medical School and tailored to improve your ability to cope with life’s challenges to keep the Peace of Mind regardless of the situation.
